# Metric Definitions

## SLA Performance (30%)
Measures whether service delivery meets contractual service-level commitments.

## Incident Resolution Speed (25%)
Measures how quickly incidents are resolved, often using MTTR.

## Customer Satisfaction (20%)
Measured through CSAT or service survey results.

## Product Adoption (15%)
Measures feature usage, active users, or utilization of strategic capabilities.

## Escalation Frequency (10%)
Measures how often accounts require elevated support or critical intervention.

## Interpretation
- 90–100 = Healthy
- 75–89 = Watch
- Below 75 = At Risk
